# Entities

**Scope:** Character and level data types, character abilities, level definitions.

**Files:** `src/entities/character.nim`, `src/entities/level.nim`, `src/systems/levels.nim`

**Description:**
Defines the 6 playable characters (Pip, Luca, Bruno, Cara, Felix, Ivy) with their unique dimensions, colors, and abilities. Defines level structure (platforms, hazards, exits, buttons, doors, narration) and all 30 level definitions across 5 acts.
