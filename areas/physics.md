# Physics

**Scope:** Gravity, collision detection, platform resolution, character-specific physics.

**Files:** `src/systems/physics.nim`

**Description:**
AABB collision detection and resolution for characters against platforms, hazards, and exits. Gravity (980 px/s²), jump velocity (-450 px/s). Character-specific physics: Pip's double jump, Luca's float/glide, Bruno's heavy weight, Cara's wall jump, Felix's extended coyote time, Ivy's graceful fall.
